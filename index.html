Hajira Jabeen, PhD

~ Research Group Leader at the University of Bonn ~

Summary

I am working as a Senior Data Scientist & Group Leader at the University of Bonn in Smart Data Analytics since 2016. I am leading the group ‘Distributed Semantics Analytics' that deals with distributed analytics, semantic web, data mining, and big data. I am actively involved in teaching, seminars and labs related to big data and analytics. I am also working on several international projects related to big data, analytics, and knowledge exchange.
